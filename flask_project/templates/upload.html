This MVP was implemented as part of the #versusvirus Hackathon. It can help the government agencies to process forms faster and free up their time for more important work.
The scope was the automatic extraction of the COVID-19 pre-registration form for short-time work in PDF format.

It can easily be extended to extract information from other forms as well.
